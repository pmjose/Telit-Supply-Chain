### 1. Current State & Pain Points

| Question | Why It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| *"Walk me through what happens when a customer asks for lot traceability on a shipment - how long does that take today?"* | Reveals manual processes, exposes pain |

### 2. Technology & Data Landscape

| Question | Why It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| *"What systems are you pulling data from today? SAP, Salesforce, PLM...?"* | Maps the integration scope |
| *"Where does your CM production data live? Do you have real-time visibility into Foxconn/Flex output?"* | Tests CM integration maturity |

| *"What's your biggest data gap today - what do you wish you could see but can't?"* | They'll tell you the #1 priority |

### 3. Business Impact & Urgency

| Question | Why It Works |
|----------|--------------|
| *"What was your most painful supply chain disruption in the last 2 years? What did it cost?"* | Creates emotional connection to problem |
| *"How much revenue are you protecting through your EOL management process? Any surprises recently?"* | Links to Product Lifecycle value |
| *"What's a certification delay cost you? Ever missed a carrier launch window?"* | Quantifies certification pain |
| *"When an automotive customer asks for IATF 16949 traceability, are you audit-ready in 24 hours?"* | Tests compliance readiness |

### 4. Organizational & Decision-Making

| Question | Why It Works |
|----------|--------------|

| *"Is there a supply chain transformation initiative already underway?"* | Tests budget availability |
| *"What's driving urgency now vs. 6 months ago?"* | Understands timing |
| *"What would success look like 12 months from now?"* | Gets them to articulate vision |

### 5. Snowflake-Specific Positioning

| Question | Why It Works |
